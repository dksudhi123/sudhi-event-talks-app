The next total solar eclipses will occur on the following dates:

*   August 12, 2026: Arctic, Greenland, Iceland, and Spain.
*   August 2, 2027: Morocco, Spain, Algeria, Libya, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, and Somalia.
*   July 22, 2028: Australia and New Zealand.
*   November 25, 2030: Botswana, South Africa, and Australia.
*   March 30, 2033: Russia and Alaska (USA).
*   March 20, 2034: Central Africa and South Asia (Nigeria, Chad, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Iran, Pakistan, India, China).
*   September 2, 2035: China, North Korea, and Japan.

For those in the contiguous United States, the next total solar eclipse will not occur until August 22, 2044 (visible in Montana and North Dakota), followed by a major cross-country eclipse on August 12, 2045.
